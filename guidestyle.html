Company Homepage Project

About

This is part of the project from Codecademy called: Company Home Page with Flexbox, and the objective is to deploy a Home page for a fictional or real company. After giving it some thought about what should I make for the project, I considered it was an awesome idea and opportunity to make a prototype landing page for my friend's business who is a successful entrepreneur.


Her company sales focus in three different categories: Food, Clothes and American products. Fortunately, I recently visited her store and remember it was separated one category per floor or section. So I came up with the idea of equally separating the website according to the categories but in a dynamic and aesthetic manner. The way I visualize the website is: neat organization, visually appealing to the visitor while scrolling down, small but strong color palette, features the uniqueness of the company, cleverly layed out.


A company I found inspiration from is Apple, and the way they display their website and their products is just brilliant. Therefore, after some investigation I wrote a piece of JavaScript to make the display of frames.
